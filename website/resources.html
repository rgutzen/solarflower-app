Key Takeaways

Quick facts to keep in mind when planning your solar journey.

Maintenance

  • Minimal upkeep required — mainly cleaning
  • Dust/debris can reduce output by 20-25%
  • Rain can help wash panels naturally
  • Annual professional inspection recommended

Financial

  • Federal incentives vary widely by region
  • Check local utility rebate programs
  • Net metering policies differ by provider
  • Payback typically 5-15 years

Technical

  • Panels last 25-30 years
  • Degradation: 0.5-0.8% per year
  • Optimal tilt ≈ latitude × 0.9 + 3°
  • South-facing (NH) / North-facing (SH)

Solar energy is more than clean electricity, it is a path toward independence.
Every rooftop panel shifts power from centralized infrastructure back to individuals and communities.
Solarflower exists to put the knowledge, tools, and confidence for that transition into everyone's hands - openly, transparently, accessibly.

Solar Panel Power

A step-by-step computational article deriving solar panel yield from first principles — solar geometry, atmospheric physics, panel temperature, and electrical modelling.
